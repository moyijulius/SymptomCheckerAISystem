About AI-Based Symptom Checker

Introduction

The AI-Based Symptom Checker is an advanced tool designed to help users identify potential diseases based on their symptoms.

By entering your symptoms, the app uses machine learning algorithms to predict possible health conditions and recommend further actions.

How It Works

The app analyzes your symptoms, matches them with a comprehensive database of diseases, and suggests possible health conditions that may match your symptoms.

AI and Machine Learning

The system uses a machine learning model trained on a vast dataset of diseases, symptoms, treatments, and medications to provide accurate predictions. The model is built using Python libraries such as scikit-learn.

Privacy and Security

Your privacy is our priority. No personal data is stored, and the app only uses the symptoms you input for processing.
